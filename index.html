We build digital things the way a sign shop builds signs, carefully, by hand, so they last. No platform fees. No outsourced soul.

Ace Digital Services is a one-person operation out of Hunterdon County, NJ. We design, build, host, and manage websites for local businesses, and keep them running so you never have to touch them. We also handle branding, social media, and photo & video content, so your whole online presence can come from one person who actually picks up the phone.

Private hosting means your site lives on a real server we own and run, not a shared GoDaddy box or a Wix/Squarespace plan with platform fees and ads piled on. No big upfront cost: one simple monthly covers the hosting and keeps your site updated, so it never goes stale, and you never have to touch it.

Custom tools are available when you want them. We can wire up scripts and integrations across the apps you already use (Gmail, Stripe, your CRM, whatever) to take repetitive busywork off your plate.
